About
I am a first year PhD student in computer science at Stanford. My research interests largely focus on distributed systems and networking. This fall, through Stanford's first year rotation program, I am working with Professor Matei Zaharia.

I have completed both my Bachelor's (2017) and my M.Eng (2018) degrees at MIT, where I researched in the Networks and Mobile Systems group in CSAIL, under Professor Hari Balakrishnan. I also collaborated closely with Professor Keith Winstein. My work focused on congestion control.
