Deepti Raghavan
About
I am a fourth year PhD student in computer science at Stanford. My research interests largely focus on distributed systems and networking. I am lucky to be advised by Matei Zaharia and Phil Levis. I also closely collaborate with Irene Zhang. I am supported by a National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship (2019) and a Stanford School of Engineering Fellowship (2018-2019).

I received both my Bachelor's (2017) and my M.Eng (2018) degrees at MIT, where I researched in the Networks and Mobile Systems group in CSAIL, under Hari Balakrishnan. My work focused on congestion control; I worked on CCP.
